How To Tame a Dragon

You Need All These Items

  • Sword
  • Dragon Food
  • Magic Wand

    Steps On Taming A Dragon

  • Aproach The Dragon
  • IF Dragon Is Not Friendly, take Sword and Wand Out.
  • If The Dragon is Close Ranged, Best if you use Wand.
  • After Dragon is Defeated and Cannot Attack Back, Feed it Some Food.
  • Take Care of it's Wounds.
  • If Dragon is Friendly, Give it food daily.
  • If You Found a Dragon Egg, take care of it like a baby.
  • Feed it Dragon Food.
